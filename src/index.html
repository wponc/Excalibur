EXCALIBUR

Integrated cloud imagery


ArcGIS Excalibur is an imagery platform for visualization and analytics through integration with ArcGIS Enterprise.

It's well-suited for the defense and intelligence community, consolidating the needs of an imagery analyst and a GIS specialist into a single cloud based solution.

Imagery specialist workflows:

- imagery search & discover
- mosaic generation
- product distribution

GIS analyst workflows:

- area calculations
- distance measurements
- basemap referencing

Functional significance


- Excalibur builds an imagery platform on top of the typical GIS kernel

- Users discover imagery through WebGIS

- Behind the scenes tie-in with ArcGIS Online

- Embedded within ArcGIS ecosystem as portal application
